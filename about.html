About Me

Allow me to introduce myself, I'm Armando J. Santiago, a curious 16-year-old hailing from the beautiful island of Puerto Rico. Since my early years, technology has always been my passion, especially video games. As a kid, I can still recall the excitement I felt when my parents suggested I attend a programming course, which would eventually set me on the path to a Computer Science career.

Thankfully, my parents were extremely supportive of my budding interest in programming, and my dad would eagerly ask about what I had learned each day. It was that encouragement and enthusiasm that fueled my passion for coding.

Over time, I continued to explore the vast world of programming, learning new languages and tools to enhance my skills. Thanks to platforms like Scratch, I was able to build my own games and watch my creations come to life.

When I enrolled in CIMATEC, my primary objective was to expand my knowledge in programming, and boy, did I achieve that goal! Not only did I get the chance to join the Robotics Club, but I also took on the role of the team's lead programmer. I even mastered the complex programming languages of Python and C++, which allowed me to create sophisticated programs that made my daily routine both efficient and enjoyable.

Looking back, it's incredible to see how far I've come in my programming journey, and I'm excited to continue exploring the endless possibilities that lie ahead.
